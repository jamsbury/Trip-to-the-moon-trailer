A Trip to the Moon is a story about a group of brave astronauts that travel to the moon. Shortly after arriving and taking in the beauty of the stars, they realize they are not alone. The film was an international success at the time of its release and was profoundly influential on later filmmakers. The innovative editing and special effects techniques became widely used and eventually became an important element within the medium of film. The has been referenced and remade, but the original still speaks for itself.
